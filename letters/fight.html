🤍 Open After We Fight

Hi love,

I know if you're reading this, we probably said things we didn’t mean. Maybe we misunderstood each other, maybe we just got overwhelmed. Whatever it was — I'm sorry. Not just for what was said, but for any way I may have made you feel hurt, distant, or unloved.

Let’s remember what we’re fighting *for*, not just what we’re fighting about. We’re on the same team. Always.

I’m here when you're ready. With softness, with patience, and with love — always.
